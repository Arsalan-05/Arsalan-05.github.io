About // engineering identity

I'm a CS student at York University building full-stack systems and AI-powered applications. My focus is the full stack — front end, back end, databases, APIs, and everything in between.

The goal is not to build AI. The goal is to be the AI full-stack software engineer who builds WITH AI — who can direct it, validate its output, integrate it into real systems, own the production consequences, and deliver what AI alone cannot: judgment, architecture, security, and shipping discipline.

AI commoditizes engineers who scaffold CRUD apps. It cannot replace engineers who understand why an architecture decision is correct, who can debug distributed systems failures, and who own production reliability.

Product engineering judgment
